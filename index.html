Our story

Born From A Grill,
Built For The Block.

RoadHouse Kitchen started as a single charcoal pit and a stubborn belief that a proper steak shouldn't need an occasion. Today we're a North Nazimabad staple — part diner, part steakhouse, part late-night pizza fix.

Everything that hits your table passes through real fire: hand-cut steaks, wood-fired pizza dough, and wings that spend real time over the coals — not a fryer.
